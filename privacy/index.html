LEDSEC.DEV

Privacy

This site uses limited first-party telemetry to understand traffic, diagnose failures, improve accessibility, and evaluate interactive work.

Recorded

  • Anonymous browser and visit identifiers
  • Pages, referrers, visit duration, scroll milestones, and link activity
  • Browser, operating system, device class, viewport, language, timezone, and accessibility preferences
  • Named events from interactive features, including the pinball game
  • IP address, coarse location, and network metadata for short-term diagnostics

Not Recorded

Form contents, raw keystrokes, pointer trails, MAC addresses, hardware serial numbers, advertising identifiers, and cross-site browsing are not collected. Telemetry is not sold or used for advertising.

Retention

Exact IP addresses are redacted after seven days. Remaining visit and activity records are deleted after 30 days. Known automation is excluded from routine reports.

Control

Global Privacy Control and Do Not Track are honored. Telemetry can also be disabled or re-enabled for this browser.
